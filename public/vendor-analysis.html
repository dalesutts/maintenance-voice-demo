Vendor Recommendation for Mynd Voice AI

RECOMMENDED: Best balance

Vapi + Deepgram + Cartesia + Claude Haiku

$0.08/min · $0.32/call
  • 20% cheaper than current Retell stack
  • Fastest latency (Cartesia built for real-time)
  • Vapi is most flexible platform (swap any component)
  • Deepgram Nova-2 is industry-standard STT
  • Good voice quality (8/10) suitable for phone calls
  • ~$640/mo at 2K calls vs $800/mo on Retell
PREMIUM: Best voice quality

Vapi + Deepgram + ElevenLabs + Claude Haiku

$0.23/min · $0.92/call
  • ElevenLabs is best-in-class voice naturalness (10/10)
  • Best for executive demos or investor presentations
  • 2.8x more expensive than recommended option
  • Slightly higher TTS latency than Cartesia
  • Voice cloning available for branded experience
  • ~$1,840/mo at 2K calls
BUDGET: Lowest cost at scale

DIY: Deepgram + AWS Polly Neural + Claude Haiku

$0.034/min · $0.14/call
  • 3x cheaper than current Retell stack
  • Requires building your own orchestration layer
  • Voice quality is "good enough" (7/10), not premium
  • More engineering investment upfront (~2-3 weeks)
  • Best unit economics at 10K+ calls/mo
  • ~$280/mo at 2K calls

Suggested path

Now (PoC validated): Stay on Retell for demos and stakeholder buy-in. $0.10/min is fine at low volume.
Q3 (production pilot): Migrate to Vapi + Deepgram + Cartesia. 20% savings, better latency, more control. Swap is straightforward since our Custom LLM server is platform-agnostic.
Q4+ (scale): Evaluate DIY stack if volume justifies the engineering investment. At 10K+ calls/mo, the savings are $460/mo vs Vapi and $660/mo vs Retell.
